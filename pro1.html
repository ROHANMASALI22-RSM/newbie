Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: approx 5 minutes
  • Preparation: approx 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, chopped vegetables, cooked meats

Instructions

  1. the eggs into a bowl. Add a pinch of salt and pepper. Beat the eggs until well combined.
  2. a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter or oil.
  3. Once the butter is melted and hot, pour in the beaten eggs.
  4. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for about 1-2 minutes until they start to set around the edges.
  5. If using fillings, sprinkle them over one half of the omelette.
  6. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the eggs are fully set and the cheese (if used) is melted.
  7. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately
